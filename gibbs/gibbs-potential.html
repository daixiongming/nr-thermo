Taking into account the relationship between the chemical excess potential and the activity coefficient in the liquid phase:

μiE=RTlnγiL

we obtain:

μ1E=RTlny1Px1P10+P-P10B11-ν10L+δ12y22P

μ2E=RTlny2Px2P20+P-P20B22-ν20L+δ12y12P

and so,

gE=x1μ1E+x2μ2E

Application

We are going to study the system 1.3-dioxolane + hexane at 308.15 K (Calvo, E.; J. Chem. Eng. Data 1999, 44, 193-196)
